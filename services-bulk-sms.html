What is bulk SMS?

Bulk SMS is a powerful communication channel that allows businesses to send messages to a large group of customers simultaneously. Over the years, SMS has made personal and marketing communication simpler, more effective, and cost-efficient. Unlike conventional marketing methods or email campaigns, SMS has higher open rates, with most messages being read within seconds of receipt. Bulk SMS helps businesses promote products, improve sales, and maintain customer relationships efficiently.
